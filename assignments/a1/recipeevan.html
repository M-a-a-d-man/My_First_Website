Sephardic Dafina


Ingredients for 4 people

  • Brisket(beef)
  • 6 Eggs
  • 4x Big fat sliced in half potatoes (could be sweet potatoes)
  • 1 can Chickpeas
  • 1x Whole yellow onion sliced in half (using both parts of the onion)
  • 2 cups Rice
  • 1 cup Water to submerge the rest of the ingredients
  • Chicken broth (chicken powder) [optional:Cumin and Paprika]

Preparations

  1. Take out "Crockpot and plug it in"
  2. Put in Chickpeas
  3. Lay the meat on a bed of Chickpeas in the Crockpot
  4. put in the chopped potatoes and sweet potatoes
  5. put in the onions
  6. put in a fully shelled (but then deshell the egg once cooked, in my opinion this is easier to eat than a shelled egg)
  7. [optional: put 2 cinnamon sticks inside, or 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder for added flavour]
  8. take a clear bag like a ziplock but unsealed
  9. put the rice, water and chicken broth in the bag
  10. tie the baggy into a knot at least 1-2 inches above the mixture
  11. place the baggy on top of the rest of the ingredients in the Crockpot
  12. (This is the sauce) Now in 1 glass of water add in 2 tablespoons of Cumin, 2 tablespoons of Paprika, [optional but recommended: 1 teaspoon of cinnamon or 1 stick of cinnamon directly in the Crockpot
  13. Mix
  14. Pour around the Dafina
  15. Then pour enough water to submerge the Dafina under the Water
  16. Put Crockpot on high and right before shabbat put the Crockpot on low to stay warm for Saturday's lunch.
  17. Remember to untie the baggy and put the stuff in the baggy in a serving plate and put all the ingredients together in the same serving plate!
  18. Enjoy!

How do you serve!

WHY DO I LIKE DAFINA?!?

I like Dafina because it is very nutritious or so our mothers tell us. Also, Dafina is the finest Saphardic traditional shabbat dish. It is very flavourful and yummy, there are not enough words to even describe its deliciousness but beware the chef is the one that has the most influence on the tastiness of Dafina.
